Frequently asked

Honest answers.

How are they shipped?

Cold-packed via 1–2 day carrier. We press, bottle, and ship the morning your order goes out.

How long do shots last?

14 days refrigerated. Freeze any you won't drink in the first week — they thaw fine.

Can I skip or cancel a subscription?

Anytime, from your Account. Click the Account button up top to manage your plan.

What if my package arrives warm?

Email hello@unfiltered.shop within 48 hours — we'll replace or refund, no questions.

Do you ship outside the US?

Not yet. We ship to the lower 48 only while we scale cold logistics.
