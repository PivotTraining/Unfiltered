Our Story

Real ingredients.
No filter.

We started Unfiltered because the wellness aisle had become a graveyard of marketing — shelf-stable shots filled with concentrates and preservatives, sold with words like "clean" and "boost." We make the opposite of that.

The kitchen

A press, a bottle, a cold pack.

Every shot is cold-pressed in a small kitchen in Brooklyn the morning your order ships. We don't HPP. We don't pasteurize. We don't extend shelf life with anything but ice.

If your order goes out Monday, the ginger was a root on Sunday night. The apples were apples a few hours before that. That's the whole brand promise — speed, not shelf-stability.

Principles

What we refuse to do.

01

No concentrates.

If it says "ginger" on the bottle, it's whole-root ginger we juiced ourselves — not a powder reconstituted with water and citric acid.

02

No HPP.

High-pressure processing kills bacteria but also flattens flavor and degrades heat-sensitive nutrients. We skip it. That's why we have to ship cold.

03

No claims we can't back up.

We won't tell you a shot cures anything. We'll tell you what's in it, how it was made, and let your body decide.

How we got here

A short timeline.

  1. 2023

    Started in a home kitchen.

    The first 200 bottles were pressed in a Brooklyn apartment and hand-delivered to 12 friends every Sunday.

  2. 2024

    Moved to a commercial space.

    Same press, same recipes — just enough room to ship to anyone, anywhere in the lower 48, cold-packed and overnight.

  3. 2025

    Launched subscriptions.

    Bi-weekly and monthly cadence. Skip, pause, swap flavors from your account — no phone calls required.

  4. 2026

    Now.

    Four shots, one kitchen, every bottle pressed within 24 hours of shipping. The plan is to keep it that way.
