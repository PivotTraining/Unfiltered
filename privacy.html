Privacy policy.

Last updated: June 1, 2026

We keep this short and human. Unfiltered collects only what we need to ship you cold-pressed shots and improve the experience.

What we collect

  • Account & order info — name, email, shipping address, payment details (processed by our PCI-compliant provider; we never store full card numbers).
  • Site analytics — anonymized page views and device info so we can spot what's broken.
  • Email engagement — so we can stop emailing you about things you don't read.

What we don't do

  • No selling your data. Ever.
  • No third-party ad tracking pixels.
  • No "engagement profiling" beyond what's needed to run the store.

Your rights

Export, delete, or correct your data anytime. Email privacy@unfiltered.shop and we'll handle it within 7 days.

Changes

If we change this materially, we'll email subscribers before it takes effect.
