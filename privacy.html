Privacy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Unfiltered is built around a simple promise: you're the user, not the product. This page explains what we collect, why, and what we don't.

What we collect

  • Your email address (for account and waitlist communications).
  • Content you post publicly on the platform.
  • Basic technical logs (IP, user-agent) retained for 30 days for abuse prevention.

What we don't do

  • No ad tracking. No third-party advertising cookies.
  • No selling, sharing, or licensing your data to third parties.
  • No behavioral profiling beyond what you explicitly opt into.

Your rights

You can export your data, delete your account, or correct your information at any time. Email privacy@unfiltered.app.

Changes

If we change this policy materially, we'll notify members by email before the change takes effect.
